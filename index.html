Our story

"Every flower we touch is chosen with love."

Kathmandu Florist was born from a deep love of nature and the Nepali tradition of celebrating life with flowers. Located in the heart of Sukedhara, we source the freshest blooms from local growers across the valley.

Whether it's a single stem or a full wedding setup, we put our heart into every arrangement — because we believe flowers are more than just decoration. They're emotion, made visible.
